Privacy Policy

MyMoneyTracker · Last updated: July 2026

The short version: MyMoneyTracker does not have a server and does not collect, store, or have access to your financial data. Your transactions live on your device, and — if you choose to enable sync — in a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive.

What data the app stores, and where

All transactions, accounts, categories, and budgets you create are stored locally on your device in a private database. The developer of this app never receives, sees, or has access to this data.

Google Sign-In and Google Sheets/Drive sync (optional)

If you choose to connect a Google account, the app requests the following permissions:

  • Drive (drive.file scope): lets the app create and manage a folder and spreadsheets in your Google Drive. The app can only see files it creates itself — it cannot browse or access any other files in your Drive.
  • Sheets (spreadsheets scope): lets the app read and write your transaction data to the spreadsheets it created, so your data can sync across your own devices.
  • Profile / email: used only to show which Google account is connected inside the app.

This data is written directly from your device to your own Google Drive using Google's APIs. It never passes through, or is stored on, any server operated by the developer.

Groups and Family features

If you create or join a shared Group or Family, a spreadsheet is created in the group creator's Google Drive and shared directly with other members' Google accounts using Google's own sharing permissions — the same way you'd share any Google Sheet. The developer has no access to this data at any point. Members you invite can see the data you choose to share with the group, exactly as described inside the app.

What we don't do

  • We don't run any backend server that stores your financial data.
  • We don't sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We don't show ads or use third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.
  • We don't have the technical ability to view your transactions, balances, or synced Google Sheets.

Your control over your data

You can disconnect your Google account at any time from within the app's settings, which stops all syncing. You can also revoke the app's access entirely at any time from your Google Account permissions page. Deleting the app from your device removes all locally stored data. Any data already synced to your Google Drive remains there under your own control, since it belongs to you.

Children's privacy

MyMoneyTracker is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
